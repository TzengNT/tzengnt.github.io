Introduction

CAN houses an array of the state-of-the-art computing and communication equipment dedicated to the support of research projects and educational activities. It has space to accommodate eight researchers, and it is used as the primary space for GRAs to conduct research activities,

CAN Lab currently has six (6) GPU workstations plus many laptops and personal computers, used extensively for active research on Large Language Model, Knowledge Graphs, Weather Prediction, and GPU Scheduling for DL. The six workstations present in CAN Lab are as follows. Two customized workstations, each housing two Nvidia RTX 6000 Ada GPUs with 48GB GDDR6X VRAM each and having 256GB DRAM One customized workstation, housing two Nvidia GeForce RTX 3090 GPUs with 24GB VRAM each and having 256GB DRAM. One Dell Precision Tower 7910 workstation, housing two Nvidia TITAN RTX GPUs with 24GB VRAM each and having 64GB DRAM. One Dell Precision Tower 7910 workstation, housing one Nvidia TITAN RTX GPU with 24GB VRAM plus one Tesla T4 with 16GB VRAM and having 64GB DRAM. One Dell Precision 7920 workstation, housing one Nvidia T400 GPU with 4GB VRAM and having 192GB VARM.
