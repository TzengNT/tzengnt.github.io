Introduction

CAN houses an array of the state-of-the-art computing and communication equipment dedicated to the support of research projects and educational activities. It has space to accommodate eight researchers, and it is used as the primary space for funded Ph.D. GRAs to conduct research activities,

CAN Lab currently has 5 servers plus many laptops and personal computers, used extensively for active research on Large Language Model, Knowledge Graphs, Weather Prediction, and GPU Scheduling for DL.
